Our Story

Every Flavor Tells a Story

Since 2016, The Marigold has been Ashiyana's top destination for fine dining, featuring an exquisite selection of North Indian, Chinese, Thai, Tandoor, Continental, and Healthy Diet cuisines. Each dish is crafted with care, delivering rich and authentic flavors.

Our premium banquet is renowned for hosting unforgettable events, combining exceptional cuisine with warm hospitality. Experience the essence of dining at The Marigold.

Special Selection

Delicious Menu

  • Butter Chicken

    Tender chicken in a rich, creamy tomato sauce, infused with spices and finished with butter. A classic delight served with naan or rice.

  • Fish Finger

    Crispy, golden-brown fish fillets, seasoned and coated in a crunchy batter. Perfectly paired with a tangy dipping sauce.

  • Paneer Amritsari

    Spiced paneer marinated and fried in a crispy gram flour batter, served with mint chutney. A flavorful treat for paneer lovers.

  • Dal Makhani

    Creamy, slow-cooked lentils enriched with butter and spices, served with naan or rice. A comforting North Indian classic.

  • Kuchi Paneer

    Zesty marinated paneer cubes grilled to perfection, served with tangy sauce. Bursting with flavor and perfect for sharing.

  • Handi Mutton

    Succulent mutton slow-cooked in a rich, aromatic gravy, served with naan or rice. A true indulgence for meat enthusiasts.
